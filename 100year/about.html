About

100 Year Films is a collaborative group of visionary storytellers, artists and thinkers who feel the power to record our world comes with the responsibility to document it.



Our mission is to curate and create a body of work reflective of the world today that will continue to resonate into the future. We celebrate and foreground these works by sharing them with the public.

As film lovers, we believe that even narrative and surreal work can serve as an important document of reality. We’re interested in powerful emotive moments that linger, unique settings and everything that makes human life distinct, beautiful, tragic, mundane, funny and sublime.
