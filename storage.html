Storage (I – III)
2016
Sound
09:06

text


In three movements, Storage (I – III) combines hard drive storage specifications, a MacBook Pro "unibody" case, a number of stones, and recordings of electronics made with telephone pickups. The piece conceives of the surface of the laptop as a site of geological excavation.
